Access Point Mobile

Date Started: May 07, 2024

Tools Used:

Description:

Access Point is a mobile application I designed during my free time as a personal passion project. It was inspired by a youth event at our church, where I began to imagine how impactful it would be if that ministry had its own app. The idea grew into a platform where faith and community could thrive in the digital space. Access Point combines the familiar features of Facebook and Instagram—such as stories, posts, and communication tools—with spiritually enriching content like biblical podcasts and devotionals. Its main purpose is to help believers connect, grow, and strengthen their relationship with God and one another. The app aims to create a safe and uplifting environment focused on glorifying God. I used Figma to design the mockup, ensuring that the layout is intuitive and accessible for users of all ages. The interface follows a minimalist approach, making it simple to navigate while still being visually appealing. I based the color palette on the logo of the ministry to maintain a strong and consistent visual identity. Through Access Point, I hope to inspire the idea of using technology as a tool to build a stronger, more connected community of faith.
