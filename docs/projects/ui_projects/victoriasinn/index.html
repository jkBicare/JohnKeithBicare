Victoria's Travelers Inn Management System

Date Started: July 05, 2024

Tools Used:

Source/Reference: https://www.figma.com/design/1u6v2WVXcTTWgDUC2GgJRt/Victoria-s-Travelers-Inn-Management-System?node-id=0-1&t=IQsQc4YVcUpeJZd8-1

Description:

Victoria's Travelers Inn Management System is a user-friendly interface I designed as a mockup for a client who commissioned the project for their upcoming system. Its purpose is to assist the inn's staff and administrators in managing daily operations such as bookings, reservations, room assignments, time tracking, invoicing, and more. The system supports two types of accounts—admin and staff—each with different levels of access and responsibilities to ensure smooth workflow and data control. I created the mockup using Figma, focusing on clear structure and functionality to guide the development phase. For the design, I followed a minimalist approach to keep navigation simple and efficient for users. I also used the official color palette of Victoria’s Travelers Inn to maintain brand consistency and deliver a familiar experience to its users. This mockup serves as a visual and functional foundation for building a reliable and organized hotel management system.
