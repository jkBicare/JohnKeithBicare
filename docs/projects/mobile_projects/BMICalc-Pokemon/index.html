BMI Calculator Pokemon Theme

Date Started: October 2024

Tools Used:

Source/Reference: https://github.com/jkBicare/BMIPokemon

Description:

The Pokémon-themed BMI Calculator is a mobile application I created during college as part of our weekly activity in Mobile Development. It was built using the .NET MAUI framework and follows the MVVM (Model-View-ViewModel) structure. The app calculates a user's Body Mass Index (BMI) based on their height and weight input. What makes it unique is the Pokémon-inspired theme, which makes the user experience more engaging and visually fun. I used Figma to design the overall layout and interface, including custom assets like buttons, icons, and backgrounds. These assets were carefully chosen to reflect a Pokémon feel while keeping the UI clean and simple. Although the app doesn’t have a specific target audience, it was primarily made for academic purposes and was submitted to our professor. The goal was to practice both UI/UX design and mobile development skills. I also learned how to bind data effectively and separate logic using the MVVM pattern. The app provides clear BMI results along with visual indicators to guide users on their health status. This project allowed me to creatively combine health-related functionality with pop culture inspiration. Overall, it reflects both my technical growth and my creativity as a student developer.
